To enjoy the free WLAN network at the Apple Store,
please register your device using your cell phone.

1. Get a passcode.

Enter your mobile number and click Get Passcode.
We’ll text a code to your cell phone.

2. Register your device.

Enter the passcode you received, then click Register.
